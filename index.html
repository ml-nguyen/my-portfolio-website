About Me

My head led me to a healthcare profession, but my heart has always been with design. After stifling my desire to create for many years, I decided to finally pursue my passion and thus, my journey into the colorful world of design began.

I’ve dabbled in many creative outlets over the years, including digital painting, calligraphy, as well as cake and macaron baking. I even helped design my own wedding dress! And contrary to most doctors, I majored in Psychology instead of Biology or Chemistry. Even as a pre-optometry student, I found it fascinating to learn the whys of human behavior.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my husband and our rescued Poodle Mix. I’m also an avid foodie slash Yelp Elite reviewer (8 years and counting!) and I shamelessly admit that I spend more money on food than anything else.
